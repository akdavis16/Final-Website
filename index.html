UX Design Project

Over the course of an entire semester, myself and two other group members worked to brainstorm, plan, gather information, develope a report, and create a final prototype of an application for your phone. Along with that was the prototype of a gadget to be used with the app.
I was an effective collaborator throughout the preperation of the report. I worked primarily on the the gadget prototype and used Figma to create flow charts and the final protype for the app we now call "Food Friend."
We placed second in the AEJMC competition for the category 2 APP – Individual/Team/Single Class – Large School.

"We created FoodFriend and its paired gadget for those, or those with loved ones, with food allergies. Shopping with allergies can be tricky, with many shopping items now containing long and hard-to-read ingredient lists with foreign-sounding ingredients. The process of figuring out what is safe for oneself to eat is difficult and tedious. Additionally, many young adults with allergies seem to struggle with navigating shopping for themselves."

Call To Action Campaign

I worked with a team of two other group members to create a promotional campaign for a local organization called "Jubilee Climate Farm."

To promote the Nurturing Leaders For Social Change Summer Camp (NLCS), we will create 3 call-to-action methods; posters, social media tactics, and a tote bag. These methods not only allow us to implement our campaign within the school building, but through external platforms like an Instagram page, a Snapchat geotag, and TikTok videos. Our call to action, #DIG-IN, will utilize hashtags within these platforms and allow students to post about their experiences from the farm. Through our call-to-action, we hope to spread Jubilee’s community message and encourage students to share their newfound skills and values from the NLSC Summer Camp about regenerative agriculture.
